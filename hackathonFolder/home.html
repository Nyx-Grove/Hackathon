Hello, world!

Help a Fellow student out

Are you a college student? Do you spend a lot of money on books which you only used 2 chapters of? Do you still have those text books and need different ones for the next semester? Bibliphile is a way you can help out other college students, get the new books you need, and get rid of the old ones you don't need.

How does it work?

Bibliphile is a used book store/excange website for college students. Studnets will be able to either buy books from others or excange for a book that the seller may need. "How do we know what books they need?" Next to the cost of the books there will be a wishlist; If you have any of the books on that wishlist you may uses that to pay instead. The books you sell and/or excange must be have all the pages and they must be readable. As long as we can still read the contents it's ok if it's a little beat up. If you're still unsure rather your book would be accpeted as payment you can also just ask the seller if it's ok with the chatting feature.

Why use Bibliphile?

There are other thrift book stores so why should you use Bibliphile? Bibliphile will not only be a book store but a study group too. On the website people will have the option to create or join a study group. These study group could help connect student that go to the same school but never talked to each other. There could also be people who passed the class or teachers who just want to help someone else, hearing somone explain it an diferent way can be really helpful. If all of this sounds amazing then come and join the community.
